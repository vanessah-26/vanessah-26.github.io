Mobile App · React Native · Feb–July 2024
Santa Cruz Mountains Art Center
Volunteer and Events Management Mobile App
React Native · Firestore · Airtable · Figma

Overview

A mobile app built to replace manual volunteer and event management workflows for the Santa Cruz Mountains Art Center, covering 7+ monthly events and saving ~15+ admin hours monthly. I owned core features including Notifications, Events, Export, and Admin management, and supported user testing.

About Nonprofit

The Santa Cruz Mountains Art Center (SCMAC) aims to support and encourage the arts through education, exhibitions, and cultural activities. The Board of Directors ensures unique art experiences via hands-on learning, artwork displays, and cultural events that enrich the lives of residents and visitors. SCMAC offers exhibit space, art classes for all ages, music, life drawing, and ceramics. It also provides opportunities for paid teachers, interns, and a variety of volunteer positions.

Problem

SCMAC has long used pen and paper for event sign-ups, leading to frequent miscommunications among volunteers and operational inefficiencies. Instances of multiple volunteers bringing unnecessary supplies highlight the need for a better system. Due to their lack of dexterity with technology, SCMAC wanted a simple, intuitive digital solution to streamline sign-ups and improve communication for their events.

Team

1 project lead · 2 designers · 6 developers

What I built

  • Push notification system for event reminders and real-time volunteer updates
  • Events management with RSVP tracking, attendance check-in, and scheduling
  • Export volunteer records and activity logs for admin reporting as Google Sheet or PDF
  • Admin management, volunteer profiles, and backend multimedia management using Firestore

Process

Worked in weekly Scrum sprints with pair programming sessions across the engineering team. Each sprint involved planning, development, and a review with the project lead and designers to validate progress against client requirements.

Outcome

The SCMAC App officially launched on the App Store on May 13, 2025. We're thrilled about its potential to enhance the experience for art gallery visitors, support volunteers, and engage participants for years to come.
